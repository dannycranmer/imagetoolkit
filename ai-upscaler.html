How it works

🧠

TensorFlow.js

Uses Google's TensorFlow.js to perform tensor-based super-resolution directly in your browser — no server required.

100% Private

Your images are processed entirely on your device. Nothing is ever sent to a server. Safe for sensitive photos.

Sharper Results

Tensor bilinear super-resolution produces sharper edges and finer detail than standard browser interpolation.
